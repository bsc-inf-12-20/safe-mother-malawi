Danger Signs
Go to clinic immediately if you notice:
🩸 Vaginal bleeding
Any bleeding during pregnancy is serious. Go to the clinic now.
🤕 Severe headache / blurred vision
May be pre-eclampsia. Seek help immediately.
👣 Baby not moving > 2 hours
Contact your clinician right away.
🦶 Swelling of face or hands
Sudden swelling may indicate a complication.
🌡️ Fever above 38°C
Could be malaria or infection. Act now.

Postnatal danger signs
9:41Danger Signs
Postnatal Danger Signs
For you and your baby — go to clinic immediately
For the mother:
🩸 Heavy or foul-smelling bleeding
More than 1 pad per hour is serious.
🌡️ Fever above 38°C
Could be infection. Do not wait — seek care.
😢 Extreme sadness or confusion
Postnatal depression is real. Tell your clinician.
For the baby:
🤧 Not feeding / very weak
A baby refusing to feed needs urgent attention.
🌡️ Temp < 36°C or > 38°C
Temperature outside this range is an emergency.
